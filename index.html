Cycling and Health

About

This thesis aims to compare localized health values with cycling factros at a Medium Super Output Area Level in London

Select any blue polygon to get a breakdown of its specific scores.

The cycling scores are obtained by using census data for number of people who report commnuting to work by bike, the crash rate is number of cycling accidents and the cycling quality index score rates the street level infrastrucre in the MSOA.
